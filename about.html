Author: Ellen Wong

 

Ellen is a fourth-year UCLA Psychology Major and Digital Humanities Minor student interested in child development. She currently volunteers at KidsConnect in the UCLA Semel Institute for Neuroscience and Human Behavior. While taking the supplementary course Disability Studies 138XP: Applied Autism Intervention: Multidisciplinary Perspective, she continues to understand the significance of Autism Spectrum Disorder awareness.
